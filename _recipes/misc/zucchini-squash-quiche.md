Zucchini Squash Quiche
Good.
Ingredients
1 c. bisquick
3 c. diced squash
1 grated carrot
1 small grated onion
½ c. grated cheese
½ t. salt
¼ t. pepper
¼ t. garlic powder
½ c. oil
4 eggs
Steps
Press in greased 8x8.
Bake at 350℉ for 1 hour.
