Cheese Ball
Written on back of a U.S. Postal Service routing slip.
Ingredients
16 oz. cream cheese
1 dry ranch-style dressing pack
bacon bits
Steps
Mix cream cheese and ranch dressing. Roll into ball. Chill. Roll in bacon bits.
