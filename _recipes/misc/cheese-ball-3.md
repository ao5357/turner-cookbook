Cheese Ball
“Here’s what’s cookin’. Recipes from the kitchen of Dolly Cz.”
Ingredients
1 c. grated cheddar cheese
8 oz. cream cheese
1 T. chopped onion
1 T. lemon juice
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
¼ lb. soft butter
Steps
Mix all ingredients. Chill. Roll in nuts.
