Cheesy Drop Biscuit
From Mom Cz.
Ingredients
2 c. flour
½ t. salt
4 T. shortening
2 t. baking powder
1 c. grated American cheese
1 c. milk
Steps
Mix all ingredients well. Bake at 450℉ for 12-15 minutes.
