Hodel Dressing
Western style.
Ingredients
¾ c. sugar
1 t. paprika
1 t. salt
1 t. minced onion
⅓ c. ketchup
⅓ c. vinegar
1 c. oil
1 t. celery salt
Steps
Combine dry ingredients and ketcup in a mixer.
Add the vinegar and oil alternately.
And the celery salt and mix.
