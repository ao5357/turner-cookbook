  - "1 lb. powdered sugar"
  - "8 oz. cream cheese"
  - "2 eggs, beaten"
---

Use a wooden spoon to mix the cake (cake mix, egg, butter). Spread into a 9x13 pan.

Mix frosting ingredients and spread over the cake. Sprinkle with crushed nuts.

Bake at 325℉ for 35-45 minutes.
