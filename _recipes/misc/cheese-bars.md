Cheese Bars
Ingredients
Cake
Butter recipe yellow cake mix
1 egg, beaten
¼ lb. butter, melted
Frosting
1 lb. powdered sugar
8 oz. cream cheese
2 eggs, beaten
Steps
Use a wooden spoon to mix the cake. Spread into a 9x13 pan.
Mix frosting ingredients and spread over the cake. Sprinkle with crushed nuts.
Bake at 325℉ for 35-45 minutes.
