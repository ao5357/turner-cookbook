Hamburger Stroganoff
From Mom Cz.
Ingredients
4 oz. egg noodles (4 c. raw)
¾ c. onion
1 lb. ground beef
1 can cream of mushroom soup
½-1 c. milk
¾ t. salt
¼ c. ketchup
2 t. Worcestershire sauce
Steps
Cook noodles. Brown onions and meat. Mix all together.
Bake at 350℉ for 25-30 minutes.
