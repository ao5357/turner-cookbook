Squash Dressing
Steve’s mother’s
Ingredients
1 ½ c. cooked squash
½ c. chopped onion
3 c. cornbread crumbs
1 stick margarine, melted
1 t. sage
2 eggs, beaten
2 cans cream of chicken soup
Steps
Combine ingredients in an 8x8 pans.
Bake at 375℉ until golden
