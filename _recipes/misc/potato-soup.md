Potato Soup
Ingredients
potatoes, pared and diced
onion, chopped
salt
water
milk
butter
black pepper
Steps
Mix potatoes, onion, and salt. Cover with water and cook until tender. Drain off water.
Cover with milk. Add chunks of butter and black pepper.
Heat to desired temperature.
