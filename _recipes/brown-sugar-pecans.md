Brown Sugar Pecans
Ingredients
1 egg white
dash of salt
¾ c. brown sugar
1 t. vanilla
2 c. pecans
Steps
Beat egg white with salt until stiff.
Add brown sugar, vanilla, and pecans.
Place separately on a greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 250℉ for 30 minutes.
Shut oven off and leave in the hot oven for 5-10 minutes more.
