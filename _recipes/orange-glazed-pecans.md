#image: filename.jpg
cookingMethod: "Baking"
recipeYield: "1 loaf"
#prepTime: "15M"
#cookTime: "1H"
#totalTime: "1H15M"
ingredients:
  - "4 c. pecan halves"
  - "½ c. frozen orange juice concentrate (undiluted)"
  - "1 ½ c. sugar"
  - "¼ t. cinnamon"
---

Bake pecans at 350℉ for 10-15 minutes.
Bring juice, sugar, and cinnamon to boil, stirring 1 minute.
Add pecans.
Drop onto foil. Let stand until firm.
