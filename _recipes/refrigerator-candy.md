Refrigerator Candy
From Mom Cz
Ingredients
2 ¼ c. sugar
¾ c. evaporated milk
⅓ c. light corn syrup
2 T. butter
12 oz. chocolate chips
1 t. vanilla
nuts (optional)
Steps
Bring to boil first four ingredients. Cook over moderate heat for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat. Add chocolate chips and vanilla.
Add nuts if desired.
Pour in 8x8 greased pan and chill.
Makes 2 ½ lb.
