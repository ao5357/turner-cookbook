ingredients:
  - "2 T. oil"
  - "¾ c. celery, finely chopped"
  - "½ c. onion, chopped"
  - "1 can salmon"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 c. evaporated milk"
  - "1 c. bread crumbs"
  - "1 t. salt"
  - "¼ t. pepper"
  - "½ c. mayonnaise"
  - "1 T. lemon juice"
  - "1 t. dill weed"
  - "¼ c. sour cream"
  - "1 T. milk"
  - "½ t. salt"
  - "½ t. sugar"
  - "⅛ t. pepper"
---

#### Loaf

Cook celery and onion in oil for about 10 minutes.
Remove from heat.

Add salmon and liquid and other ingredients. Mix well.
Bake in a greased loaf pan at 350℉ for 50 minutes.

#### Sauce

Combine ingredients (mayo, lemon juice, dill, sour cream, milk, salt, sugar, 
pepper). Chill.
