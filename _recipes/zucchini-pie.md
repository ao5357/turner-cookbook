Zucchini Pie
Ingredients
4 c. raw zucchini, sliced thin
½ t. salt
¼ c. flour
2 eggs, beaten
1 ¼ c. sugar
1 ½ t. apple pie spice (or cinnamon and nutmeg)
¼ c. lemon juice
Steps
Combine all ingredients. Stir well. Pour into unbaked pie crust.
Bake at 375℉ for 1 hour.
