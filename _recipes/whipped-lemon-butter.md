ingredients:
  - "½ c. soft butter"
  - "1 T. minced parsley"
  - "1 t. chopped chives"
  - "½ t. salt"
  - "dash cayenne pepper"
  - "3 T. lemon juice"
---

Cream butter. Add parsley, chives, salt, and cayenne. Add lemon juice about 
¼ at a time.
Can store in refrigerator in a covered jar.
