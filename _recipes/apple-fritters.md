Apple Fritters
Ingredients
1 c. sifted flour
1 t. powdered sugar
1 t. baking powder
¼ t. salt
1 beaten egg
3 diced apples
¼ c. milk
Steps
Mix ingredients in order. Drop by teaspoonfuls in hot oil until brown. Shake on powdered sugar.
