Rice Pudding
From a friend. Good.
Ingredients
3 c. cooked rice
1 stick butter
2 c. sugar
1 ½ c. milk
3 eggs
2 t. vanilla
Steps
Cook rice and add butter.
Spray pan with Pam.
Combine ingredients and add to rice.
Bake at 350℉ for 1 hour.
