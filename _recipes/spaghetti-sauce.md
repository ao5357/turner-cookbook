#image: filename.jpg
cookingMethod: "Baking"
recipeYield: "1 loaf"
#prepTime: "15M"
#cookTime: "1H"
#totalTime: "1H15M"
ingredients:
  - "1 T. shortening"
  - "1 lb. ground beef"
  - "1 onion, chopped"
  - "½ c. celery with leaves"
  - "2 T. parsley flakes"
  - "12 oz. tomato paste"
  - "16 oz. tomato sauce"
  - "½ t. salt"
  - "¼ t. pepper"
  - "1 clove garlic, minced"
  - "½ t. Italian seasoning"
  - "1 bay leaf"
  - "¾ c. water"
---

Saute celery and onions in shortening. Add meat and brown.
Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 3-4 hours, adding water as needed.
Can be frozen.
