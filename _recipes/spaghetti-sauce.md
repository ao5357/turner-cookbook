Spaghetti Sauce
From Mom Cz. Delicious.
Ingredients
1 T. shortening
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, chopped
½ c. celery with leaves
2 T. parsley flakes
12 oz. tomato paste
16 oz. tomato sauce
½ t. salt
¼ t. pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
½ t. Italian seasoning
1 bay leaf
¾ c. water
Steps
Saute celery and onions in shortening. Add meat and brown.
Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 3-4 hours, adding water as needed.
Can be frozen.
