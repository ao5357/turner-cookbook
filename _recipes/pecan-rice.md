ingredients:
  - "1 ½ c. brown rice"
  - "3 T. butter"
  - "1 chopped onion"
  - "1 c. pecans"
  - "¼ c. parsley"
  - "1 T. ginger"
  - "1 T. basil or ½ t. dried basil"
  - "salt and pepper"
---

Saute onion. Add cooked rice.
Add pecans, herbs, and seasonings.
Stir and simmer 10 minutes.
