Potato Chip Cookies
Very good. My Mom saved potato chip crumbs in freezer until she had enough.
Ingredients
1 c. shortening
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. sugar
2 eggs
2 c. flour
½ t. salt
1 t. soda
1 t. vanilla
2 c. crushed potato chips
1 c. nuts
Steps
Drop by teaspoonfuls on ungreased sheet.
Bake at 350℉ for 12 minutes.
