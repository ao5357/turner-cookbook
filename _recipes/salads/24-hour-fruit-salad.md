24 Hour Fruit Salad
From Mom Cz.
Ingredients
3 eggs, beaten
¼ t. mustard
½ t. salt
¼ c. lemon juice
1 pt. cream (whipped)
25 marshmallows
1 large can pineapple
1 large can fruit cocktail
extra cherries as desired
Steps
Add mustard, salt, and lemon juice to the eggs. Cook until thick. Let cool.
Add rest of ingredients. Let stand for 24 hours.
