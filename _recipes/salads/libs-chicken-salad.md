Lib’s Chicken Salad
Ingredients
2 c. chicken, cooked and cubed
1 ½ c. celery, cut
1 ½ c. white seedless grapes
1 ½ c. cubed pineapple
1 ½ c. toasted almonds
½ c. mayonnaise
¼ c. sour cream
1 t. curry
1 t. lemon juice
1 t. salt
Steps
Mix together mayo, sour cream, curry, lemon juice, and salt.
Then mix all ingredients together.
Serve on lettuce.
