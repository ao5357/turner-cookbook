title: "Gingersnaps"
description: "My Mom’s recipe"
category: "Desserts"
#image: filename.jpg
cookingMethod: "Baking"
recipeYield: "1 loaf"
#prepTime: "15M"
#cookTime: "1H"
#totalTime: "1H15M"
ingredients:
  - "¾ c. shortening"
  - "1 c. sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "¼ c. molasses"
  - "2 c. flour"
  - "¼ t. salt"
  - "2 t. soda"
  - "1 t. cinnamon"
  - "½ t. cloves (ground)"
  - "½ t. ground ginger"
---

Mix all ingredients. Roll into balls.
Roll in white sugar.
Flatten balls with fork.
Bake at 375℉ for 10 to 15 minutes.
