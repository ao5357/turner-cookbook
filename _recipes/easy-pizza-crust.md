Easy Pizza Crust
This is a batter to pour into a cookie-type pan with sides.
Ingredients
1 c. flour
1 t. salt
1 t. Italian seasoning or leaf oregano
⅛ t. pepper
1 egg
⅔ to ¾ c. milk
Steps
Grease and light dust pan.
Bake at 425℉ for 10 to 15 minutes or until brown.
