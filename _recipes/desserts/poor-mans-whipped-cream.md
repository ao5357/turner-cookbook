Poor Manâ€™s Whipped Cream
Mom Cz.
Ingredients
1 c. milk
4 T. flour
1 c. oleo
1 c. sugar
2 t. vanilla
Steps
Cook milk and flour until like a paste. Set aside.
Beat oleo for 4 minutes, then add the sugar and vanilla. Beat for 4 more minutes.
Add the paste and beat for another 4 minutes.
Chill.
