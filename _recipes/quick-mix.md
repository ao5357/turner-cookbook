Quick Mix
Like bisquick.
Ingredients
8 ½ c. flour
3 T. baking powder
1 T. salt
2 t. cream of tartar
1 t. soda
1 ½ c. instant nonfat dry milk
2 ¼ c. vegetable shortening
Steps
Mix dry ingredients. Cut in shortening.
Store in cool dry place. Use within 4 months.
