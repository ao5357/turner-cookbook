  - "2 eggs"

  - "1 t. vanilla"
  - "1 ½ t. vegetable oil"
  - "¼ c. buttermilk"
  - "1 t. cinnamon"
  - "2 c. pecans"
  - "1 c. pears, canned, finely chopped"

Bake at 325℉ for 1 hour
