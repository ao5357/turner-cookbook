ingredients:
  - "¾ c. butter"
  - "1 ½ c. sugar"
  - "4 oz. canned milk"
  - "6 oz. chocolate chips"
  - "½ jar marshmallow creme"
  - "dash salt"
---

Boil the butter, sugar, and milk for 5 minutes. Add the chocolate chips, creme, and salt. Allow chocolate to melt.
Cool and chill.
