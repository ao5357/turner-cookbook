#prepTime: "15M"

ingredients:
  - "2 c. biscuit mix"
  - "½ c. shredded cheddar cheese"
  - "½ c. beer"
---

Mix biscuit mix and cheese. Make a well in center and add beer. Stir just until 
clings together.

Knead on flour surface 5 times. Roll or pat into rectangle. Cut into triangles 
and bake on an ungreased sheet.

Bake at 450℉ for 8-10 minutes.
