ingredients:
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "¾ c. sugar"
  - "1 c. nut meats"
  - "1 c. chocolate chips"
---

Preheat over to 350℉.
Beat egg whites until stiff. Add sugar slowly.
Add nuts and chips.

Drop by teaspoon-ful on parchment paper.
Put in over and immediately turn oven off.
Leave 3 hours or overnight.
