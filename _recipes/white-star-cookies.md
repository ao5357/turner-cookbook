ingredients:
  - "12 oz. white stars"
  - "2 T. peanut butter"
  - "1 c. mini marshmallows"
  - "1 c. peanuts"
  - "1 c. rice krispies"
---

Melt white stars and peanut butter in a double boiler. Remove from heat and add other ingredients.
Drop by Tablespoon on wax paper.
