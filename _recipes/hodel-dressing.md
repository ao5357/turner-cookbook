ingredients:
  - "¾ c. sugar"
  - "1 t. paprika"
  - "1 t. salt"
  - "1 t. minced onion"
  - "⅓ c. ketchup"
  - "⅓ c. vinegar"
  - "1 c. oil"
  - "1 t. celery salt"
---

Combine dry ingredients and ketcup in a mixer.
Add the vinegar and oil alternately.
And the celery salt and mix.
