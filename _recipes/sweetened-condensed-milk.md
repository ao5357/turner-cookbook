ingredients:
  - "1 c. and 2 T. instant dry milk"
  - "½ c. warm water"
  - "¾ c. sugar"
---

Place water in bowl. Add dry milk and mix well. Add sugar and mix until smooth. Set bowl in pan of hot water.
Store in glass jars.
