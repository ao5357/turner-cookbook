ingredients:
  - "9 potatoes (approximately)"
  - "6 oz. cream cheese"
  - "1 c. sour cream"
  - "2 t. onion powder"
  - "1 t. salt"
  - "¼ t. pepper"
  - "2 T. butter"
---

Boil potatoes in salted water. Mash potatoes and add rest of ingredients.
Refrigerate until ready to bake.

Spread into a lightly-greased baking dish. Bake at 350℉ for 30 minutes.
